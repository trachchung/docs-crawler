## Custom domain
The **Custom Domain** module in the Speed serves as a powerful tool for businesses seeking to elevate their brand presence in the online payment landscape. By providing users with the ability to customize their payment experience through a dedicated domain, this feature not only imparts a sense of exclusivity but also reinforces brand identity. This feature adds a layer of branding and professionalism, allowing businesses to seamlessly integrate the payment process into their existing online presence.
The custom domain functionality can be easily configured through three primary methods:
  1. **Email** : When Speed sends notifications to your customers, it sends them from the **tryspeed.com** domain by default. We offer a feature that allows you to create your own custom domain. Your customers will receive emails from a brand domain email address.
  2. **Checkout** : You can specify a brand domain on the payment page from which your customers will send payments. When Speed payment resources like payment links, checkout links, and others are created, your customer is directed to a payment page with the **checkout.tryspeed.com** domain by default. Change this default domain into your custom domain for a branded URL experience.
  3. **Payment address** : The system allows users to receive multiple payments using either the default domain **tryspeed.com** or their custom payment domain, if added. This flexibility and personalization enhance the user experience and facilitate seamless transactions, making Speed a user-centric and efficient payment platform.
