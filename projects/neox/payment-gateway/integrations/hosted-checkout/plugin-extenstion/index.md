NeoX Plugin provides entire payment solution
  * Payment by domestic ATM card including 42 banks in Vietnam
  * Payment by Visa / Master / JCB international cards.
  * Pay QR Code by Mobile Banking application including 37 banks in Vietnam
  * Payment by NeoX Wallet


### 
Woocommerce Integration
**Step 1:** Customers login to the admin page, then go to the plugins' menu to install.
**Step 2:** Click on **Add new and** search plugin with "**neo** " keyword
**Step 3:** Choose the **NeoX Payments for WooCommerce** plugin, then click on **Install Now**
**Step 4:** Customers click on **Active**
**Step 5** : Click on **Settings** of**NeoX Payment for WooCommerce** plugin
**Step 6:** Enable**"Add the NeoX Payment Gateway",** then click on**"Checkout"**
**Step 7:** Customers fill complete information as below image
**Note** :
**Enable/Disable** : Allow to display NeoX payment gateway on website.
**NeoPay Sanbox** : Allow to check on Sanbox environment.
**Return Page** : The page to return to after paying successful.
To get merchant information, customers can click on the link [herearrow-up-right](https://merchant.neopay.com.vn/portal/my-profile).
**Merchant Code** : get the merchant information on merchant portal.
**Secret key:** on Payment gateway configuration section under Payment page config (Merchant portal)
### 
Shopbase Integration
**Step 1:** Choose **Settings** section, then click on **Payment providers**
**Step 2:** Choose Alternative methods, then click on **Choose alternative provider**
**Step 3** : Choose **NeoX**
**Step 4:** Merchants fill complete information as below image
To get merchant information, customers can click on the link [herearrow-up-right](https://merchant.neopay.com.vn/portal/my-profile).
**Merchant Code** : get the merchant information on merchant portal.
**Secret key**(_Hashkey_): on Payment gateway configuration section under Payment page config (Merchant portal)
**Step 5:** Click on **Add account** , then verify your account (as below image)
**Note** :
**Merchant Code** : get the merchant information on merchant portal.
**Secret key:** on Payment gateway configuration section under Payment page config (Merchant portal)
