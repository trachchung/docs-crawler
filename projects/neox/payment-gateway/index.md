**Payment Gateway**
### 
Key benifits

  * Offer full packaged digital payment solutions for businesses/ Private Merchants​.
  * 100% Online registration, no technical complications, and straight into user after 30 minutes.​


NeoX Payment Gateway is the simplest integration method for web-base payment applications

  * Bank Transfer
