**Collection** is a service that a Merchant (individuals or organization) permits to collect contractual payments. These collecting authorizations are explicitly established in certain documents.
There are two types of collection: online collection and offline collection:
  * **Offline collection:** comprises services such as transportation service collection, delivery and collection of COD payments...
  * **Online collection:** comprises services such as bank transfer, pay electricity, water, and other utility bills...
