### 
Create Disbursement Request
**Step 1:** On the right panel**,** choose the **Disbursement request** under Disbursement service**,** then click on **Create Disbursement Request** button.
**Step 2** : Download the template file.
**Step 3:** Create the request file, then submit that file.
  * Make a new copy of "template_ycch" file, then fill in all columns.
  * The **BankID** field can be referred to the **Swift code** in the **List Bank** sheet.


### 
Manage the disbursement request.
  * On the list of disbursement requests, click on "**Eye** " icon to navigation to the disbursement request detail page
