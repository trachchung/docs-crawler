**Merchant Portal** is a website for NeoX partners. Support for partners in account management, transaction management, payment gateway information, making it easier to manage and solve problems quickly. 
The services Merchant Portal provides to users include:
  * Business information management
  * Manage and decentralize sub -account
  * Trading history management
  * Control management
  * Trade and transaction statistics
  * Manage household expenditure
  * Send support request.
