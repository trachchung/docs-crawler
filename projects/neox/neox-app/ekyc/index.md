**eKYC (Electronic Know Your Customer)** is an electronic customer identification solution that allows a financial institution to identify customers 100% online based on biometric information (biometrics), customer identification by artificial intelligence (AI). , without the need to meet face-to-face like the current process.
### 
Key benifits
  * Minimize time and effort when verifying identity.
  * Save costs, increase working efficiency and create conditions for financial institutions to better serve and take care of customers.
  * eKYC contributes to meeting the digitization criteria of financial services and building a unified customer information database.
