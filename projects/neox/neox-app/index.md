**NeoX App** is an application for e-wallet users and NeoX merchants. Support for users in performing e-wallet transactions (Cashin, Cashout, Transfer, Payment), and help Merchant manage revenue more conveniently and easily on the phone.
The services NeoX App provides include:
  * **E-Wallet:** Cashin, Cashout, Transfer, Payment.
  * **Merchant Management:** Manage payment transactions and store revenue.
