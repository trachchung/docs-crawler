# Universal Daemonization + Augmented Reality
May 31, 2015

Source: _[Millions of People Around You Are Playing an Alternate Reality Game You Can’t See](https://www.mic.com/articles/119366/one-million-people-around-you-are-playing-an-alternate-reality-game-you-can-t-see?utm_source=danielmiessler.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=universal-daemonization-augmented-reality) >_
This is the future of technology. It’s all about the complete blending of technology into the real world, and vice versa. 
People want to be superheroes and princesses and explorers and athletes, and technology will increasingly allow us to become these things. 
This will come from Universal Daemonization, from completely immersive games that are eventually taken over by simulations that the player cannot distinguish from reality, and eventually by moving the mind into permanent instances of these types of reality. 
I think people will play multiple games with reality as the canvas. Through Universal Daemonization and augmented reality, we’ll be able to see people who have a high rudeness rating as monsters, or people who are rated as beautiful by their friends as goddesses, regardless of their external appearance. 
We’ll see humor and status and wealth and curiosity in tangible ways like never before, and the environment will suddenly gain a fourth dimension of experience and history, with people able to see kisses and fights and murders that have happened in the exact locations they pass by. 
Augmented reality, powered by Universal Daemonization, will soon be upon us, and it’ll be extraordinary.
