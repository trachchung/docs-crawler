# AI's Total Addressable Market
Why AI is far bigger than most people think
January 28, 2025
[ #ai](https://danielmiessler.com/archives/?tag=ai)[ #technology](https://danielmiessler.com/archives/?tag=technology)[ #top](https://danielmiessler.com/archives/?tag=top)
 OASIS-logging-in…
The total market size (TAM) for AI is a combination of two (2) primary components: 
  1. The total cost of human workforces
  2. The amount of money that current and future companies will pay to start, 10x, or 1000x their business


We're talking _hundreds of trillions_ of dollars.
Don't get distracted.
