# The Most Important Sentence
I've noticed that people who can fill in this sentence are much happier than those who can't

For anyone struggling career or purpose-wise, I have a diagnostic for you.
_Fill in this sentence._
I believe one of the biggest problems in the world is ___________, which is why I'm building/creating/doing ____________.the sentence template  
And now that you’ve filled it in…are you happy with your answer?
## A lack of clarity [​](https://danielmiessler.com/blog/the-most-important-sentence#a-lack-of-clarity)
In my opinion, people are struggling with life direction and satisfaction because they can't fill out this sentence in a way that they respect.
Or—even worse— _they can't fill it out at all_.
Many are working in crushing 9-5 jobs that destroy the soul, make them dread Mondays, and that don’t fill in this sentence in a [satisfactory way](https://danielmiessler.com/blog/plan-career-around-problems).
Everything builds off of how you are useful to the world. 
## Fill yours in [​](https://danielmiessler.com/blog/the-most-important-sentence#fill-yours-in)
So, my advice to you is simple: **Write your own version of this sentence**. Make it a priority.
Once you have your sentence, you will have found a molten core for not just a thriving career—but perhaps a life as well.
Find your meaning by finding a meaningful problem and a path to solving it.
Fill in your sentence.
